From Business Data to Business Decisions

My career began in business data operations, where I spent over seven years ensuring the quality, accuracy, and reliability of data used for retail intelligence and advertising analytics. Today, I'm focused on bridging business needs with technology to help organizations improve processes, optimize performance, and make informed decisions.
